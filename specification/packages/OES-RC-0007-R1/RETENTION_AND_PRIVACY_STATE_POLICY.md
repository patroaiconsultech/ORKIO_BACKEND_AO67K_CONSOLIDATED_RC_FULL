# Retention and Privacy State Policy

OES-010 inherits the OES-008 deny-by-default privacy boundary.

## Rules

1. Raw private founder source content must not be stored in state.
2. Google Drive partial sources remain metadata-only without content access.
3. Learning signals may be stored only as sanitized operational metadata.
4. State retention must be defined before runtime persistence exists.
5. Deletion and archival must be auditable.

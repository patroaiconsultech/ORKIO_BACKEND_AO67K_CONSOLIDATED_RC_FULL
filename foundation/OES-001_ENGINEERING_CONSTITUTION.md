# OES-001 — Engineering Constitution

Version: 0.1-rc-r3
Status: RELEASE CANDIDATE
Owner: Orkio Engineering Office
Approver: Vision Owner + Independent Engineering Auditor
Depends on: none

## Objective
Define permanent engineering principles for specifying, implementing, auditing, releasing, and evolving Orkio.

## Scope
Applies to structural engineering work in specification, runtime, backend, frontend, knowledge, governance, and delivery artifacts.

## Out of Scope
Implementation details, APIs, database schemas, UI behavior, provider selection, and deployment infrastructure.

## Articles
1. Architecture precedes implementation.
2. Contracts precede integration.
3. Institutional knowledge belongs to governed Orkio assets.
4. Preserve before replace.
5. Critical legal, financial, operational, or strategic commitments require explicit human approval.
6. Auditability is required by default.
7. Engineering packages must be non-destructive.
8. Orkio must remain model-independent.
9. Quality is a process, not a text style.
10. Major deliveries should capture lessons learned.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Principles are stable and implementation-independent.
- No article grants autonomous authority for critical real-world commitments.
- The document can govern future OES modules.

## Risks
- Broad principles may become decorative unless enforced by audit gates.

## Next Steps
Use as normative foundation for future OES modules.
